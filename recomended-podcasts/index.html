Podcasts Recommendations

Kieron
 2022-08-09  272 words   2 minutes 
Contents
  • An unorganized list of podcasts I listen to:

Pocasts are easily my primary medium for information consumption. If I’m talking to you about something I recently learned, it’s probably from one of these pods. These are the podcasts that are in my regular rotation.

An unorganized list of podcasts I listen to:

  • The Bulwark Podcast A daily center right politics podcast with guests from across the political spectrum, discussing the day’s news.
  • The DishCast with Andrew Sullivan A great interviewer, hit and miss for me depending on guest
  • State of Demand Gen B2B Marketing podcast, looking beyond standard attribution models
  • Pivot Business and current events conversation
  • The Tim Ferriss Show Lifestyle topics. I always learn something new
  • WTF With Marc Maron A comedian hosting mostly serious interviews
  • Finding Mastery Sports psychologist interviewing high performers in a variety of fields
  • Hacks On Tap A Democratic and a Republican political strategist discussing current events with a variety of guests
  • Off Menu with Ed Gamble and James Acaster Two comedians interviewing various celebs about their ideal meals
  • Deep Questions with Cal Newport Ideas about technology and how to lessen the negative impact
  • DataFramed Interesting data science topics
  • The Knowledge Project All over the place. Always an interesting guest, although I have often never heard of them before
  • Impact Theory with Tom Bilyeu Guest / Topic dependent. Lifestyle design mostly
  • Good One, a podcast about jokes A funny chap interviews comedians about their favourite jokes and the roots to their comedy
  • The Rich Roll Podcast Vegan endurance athlete interviews a variety of high performers about what makes them tick
  • The Next Leval Three of the principal Bullwark contributors discuss the news of the week
